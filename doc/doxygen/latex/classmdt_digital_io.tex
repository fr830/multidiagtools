\subsection{Detailed Description}
Representation of a digital I/O. 

On wide range of automation applications, it is usefull to display a digital I/O to the user. For example, a digital I/O can be displayed by using this class together with \hyperlink{classmdt_digital_in_widget}{mdtDigitalInWidget} or \hyperlink{classmdt_digital_out_widget}{mdtDigitalOutWidget}
